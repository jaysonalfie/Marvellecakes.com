Our Story

A small Nairobi kitchen, an obsession with craft, and a belief that a cake should be the most memorable thing on the table.

Where it began

Marvelle Cakes was founded on a simple frustration: too many celebration cakes looked beautiful in photos but tasted forgettable in person. We set out to fix that — one fluted buttercream, one cascade of fresh fruit, one perfectly tempered chocolate shell at a time.

What started as weekend orders from a home kitchen in Westlands has grown into a full studio bakery serving weddings, milestone birthdays, and corporate events across Nairobi. The recipes have been refined; the obsession with detail hasn't changed.

Every cake that leaves our kitchen is signed off by the head baker. Every flavour is tasted before it leaves the door. That's the Marvelle standard, and we keep it without exception.
